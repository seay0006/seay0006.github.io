About Me

My name is Colin Seay, I am the first born and eldest of a family of farmers. I was born and raised just south east of a small town in rural ottawa called sarsfield. My family raised cattle and did cash cropping throught most of my young life, however after my parents split I ended up no longer in a farming environment and instead living split between Limoges and Orleans. The contrast between city life and country life was quite jaring as a young kid, firstly being able to see other people all the time was something that I didnt know I wanted until I had recieved it and by that same testament the amount of stores and shops active was mind bogling.
